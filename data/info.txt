Parameter specs:

    - Weight_repartition: Front-rear weight distribution (0 = fully front, 1 = fully rear)
    - Cx: Aerodynamic drag coefficient
    - Front_Clift:  Front aerodynamic lift coefficient
    - Rear_Clift": Rear aerodynamic lift coefficient
    - Front_wing_angle: Front wing angle (degrees)
    - Rear_wing_angle: Rear wing angle (degrees)
    - Front_axle_inertia: Front axle rotational inertia (kg * m2)
    - Rear_axle_inertia: Rear axle rotational inertia (kg * m2)
    - Wheel_toe: Wheel toe angle (degrees)
    - Wheel_stiffness: Wheel stiffness (N/mm)
    - Front_wheel_camber: Front wheel camber angle (degrees)
    - Rear_wheel_camber:  Rear wheel camber angle (degrees)
